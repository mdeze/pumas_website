Coaches Registration

USAV is the governing body of New England club volleyball, and holds our insurance policy. So everyone must be registered with USAV before participating in any club volleyball activity.

I am a Returning Coach from Last Year

As a returning coach, you should already have a NERVA login.

  1. Go to the USAV web site
  2. Enter your username and password. If you don't remember your username or password, there is a link to click so they can email it to you.
  3. Select Renew Online on the left side
  4. Double check the information on the first page is correct select Continue
  5. In the upper right corner of Member Information section, select Pumas Volleyball from the pulldown for Club
  6. Do not select any additional fees, other than the registration and the background check.
  7. The system will ask you to agree to have a background if you didn't do one last year (renewal is every two years).
Notes
  • Paper forms are not required; your electronic agreement is binding.
  • You will need to pay by credit card online! We will reimburse you in your coaches stipend at the end of the season.

I am a New Coach

As a new coach, you must register with NERVA and setup a login.

  1. Go to the USAV web site
  2. Fill out all the information required carefully and submit the form. You will receive an email with your login information. Keep these in a safe place you will need them every year.
  3. This system was designed to register adult Yankee players, so it may ask questions that don't apply. Do the best you can.
  4. In the upper right corner of the second page, make sure to select Andover Pumas or Pumas from the pull-down menu for Club. Do not leave it as Undecided.
  5. Do not select any additional fees, other than the registration and the background check, unless you want to pay for them yourself.
  6. The system will ask you to agree to a background check. You must agree, you will not be allowed to coach without a background check.
Notes
  • Paper forms are not required; your electronic agreement is binding.
  • You will need to pay by credit card online! We will reimburse you in your coaches stipend at the end of the season.
