USAV Registration

USAV is the governing body of New England club volleyball. The membership fee is comprised of USA Volleyball membership ($22.50) and Regional Membership ($22.50). This covers everything from National teams to player insurance on the regional and national level. On the Regional level the fee goes to programming and development of coaches and players. Our region does not have an office or paid staff so the fees go back to programming for the sport.

Starting with our 2014 season you must pay online when you register or re-register your USAV membership. Pumas will not pay your USAV membership and no player will be allowed to tryout without their current USAV card.

Instructions

  • You will need a parent or guardian with you, to provide a driver's license or state ID number. This is used instead of a parent's signature on paper.
  • You will need a credit card to pay online.
  • Proceed to section A or B below.

A. I have played for a JO club in the past two seasons

If you have played for a Junior Olympics (JO) club (any JO club, not just Pumas), you should already have a NERVA login.

  1. Copy the USAV Membership Login URL: https://webpoint.usavolleyball.org/wp15/IntraLock/Login.asp, open a new tab or browser window and paste it to go to the USAV web site.
  2. Enter your username and password. If you don't remember your username or password, there is a link to click so they can email it to you. Keep these in a safe place, you'll need them every year.
  3. Click on "Renew Online" on the left.
  4. On the second page, in the top right, select Pumas Volleyball Club from the pulldown for Club unless you are undecided (trying out for multiple clubs), in which case select "UNDECIDED". If you choose UNDECIDED you will have to change it later when you decide which club to play for.
  5. Print your card - go to the menu on the left side of the page and click on "Print Membership Card".

B. I have not played for any JO club in the past two seasons

  1. Copy the USAV Membership Join URL: https://webpoint.usavolleyball.org/wp/memberships/join.wp?mbr_ChapterID=25971, open a new tab or browser window and paste it to go to the USAV web site.
  2. Fill out all the information required and submit the form. You will receive an email with your login information. Keep these in a safe placeas you will need them every year.
  3. Click on "Renew Online" on the left.
  4. On the second page, in the top right, select Pumas Volleyball Club from the pulldown for Club unless you are undecided (trying out for multiple clubs), in which case select "UNDECIDED". If you choose UNDECIDED you will have to change it later when you decide which club to play for.
  5. Print your card - go to the menu on the left side of the page and click on "Print Membership Card".
