12U COED: Fall Program

Pumas will be starting our COED 12U Fall Volleyball Program on October 11th. The program will be open to boys and girls who are 12 or younger who are interested in learning to play volleyball. Participants will be split into groups by ages and/or ability if possible:

  • 7-8
  • 9-10
  • 11-12
Groups may be combined if numbers require.

General Information

Program Outline

There will be two four-week sessions. The first session will start on 10/11/20. The second session will start on 12/6/20. During practice players will be using an age appropriate ball and net height. We want to pass on our love of the game while they learn the skills in a game-like environment.

Practices

Practices will be on Sundays for 1 hour and 10 minutes. Practice times will be 8:20 am - 9:30 am. We will practice at Lowell Catholic High School. We will practice at Lowell Catholic High School. Directions to Pumas facilities.

Tryouts & Registration

There are no tryouts for this program. All interested players are welcome to join. There will be a limit of 20 players. You do not have to register for both sessions at once. You can register at here.

Costs

Cost is $100 per player, per session. Payment can be made online during registration. Offline payments may be made by check. Please address the check to PVC and mail to:

12U COED Team Program

What to do after the fall program - For those players who like the sport and want to continue we have a COED Triple Ball program. This Program runs from the end of December to the beginning of May. Practices would be once a week on Sundays and tournaments would run from March to May with 5 Single day events. There are no tryouts for this program and all ages 8-12 are welcome. Players must be 12 or under on 6/31/21 to be eligible to play for this program. Space is limited. See 12U COED Team Programs for more information.

USA Volleyball Membership

All players will need a USAV membership to participate in the season and attend any sessions. This is from the National Governing Body of our sport and does not go to Pumas. There are two memberships available:

  • Full membership – Cost is $60 and is good until 6/31/21
  • Tryout membership – Cost is $15 and is a limited membership for practices only. This membership is good until 12/31/20. You can upgrade this to a full membership for an additional $50 and it would be good for the rest of the season.

If you are planning on doing the 12U COED Team Program then you would need a full membership. Register with USAV - Follow all direction on the region web site as it is a new process this year.
