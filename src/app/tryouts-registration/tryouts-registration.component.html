Open & Club Program Tryout Registration

Pumas believe that the ideal experience for the athlete, and the club, is for the athlete to play at their appropriate age group. Refer to the Frequently Asked Questions for a discussion on playing-up an age group and Tryout Guidelines for a guide to skill and experience levels for each division and age group.

Players are strongly encouraged to plan on attending all appropriate tryout sessions, but must attend at least one. Comprehensive data is collected from each tryout and used to make final decisions on team placements and cuts. The tryouts are competitive, especially at the older divisions, and cuts will be made. See FAQ for missing a tryout date.

Any athlete who does not make an Open team will be considered for the Club division automatically at no additional fee. Refer to the Tryout Schedule to determine times and locations of the tryouts for your age group and selected division. Refer to Frequently Asked Questions for a discussion on playing-up.

There are several important steps you must follow to ensure you are ready to tryout and become a player on a Pumas volleyball team.

Tryout Registration Process

Registration Notes

  • USAV membership registration can be done now
  • Pumas Registration web site will be open by 10/8
  1. Determine where you fit in the program:
    • What age group are you in? Age Group Tester
    • Do you want to play in the Club or Open division?
  2. Register (join or renew) with USAV and print out your USAV card
  3. Completely fill out a USAV medical form
  4. Register with Pumas and pay tryout fee at SportNgin
    • Download this worksheet to get your tournament availability responses ready before you register.
  5. Show up for tryouts with all of the following:
    • $150.00 Season Deposit (refer to Program Costs for more information)
    • Completely filled out medical form from step 3
    • Copy of your USAV membership card from step 2 - print your card
