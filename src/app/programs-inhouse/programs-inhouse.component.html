In-House Skills Program

Our In-house Skills Program is for those for those players that want to practice but not travel for tournaments. There will be one 6 week session with each practice lasting 2 hours. There are no tryouts for this program and all are welcome. Players on competitive teams, who want to use this opportunity to get more practice time, can register as well. We are limited in court space so registration will be stopped when we are full. Practices will be on Sundays with time and location TBD.


What will be Covered:
Most of the time will be spent on individual skills (passing, serving, setting, hitting) and small amount will be on game skills during scrimmage (rotations, position responsibilities, game play).
Session Groups:
Will be grouped by age. Adjustments may be made according to current skill level. There will be up to 15 athletes per court.
Cost:
$200 for the session. This covers the cost of court and coach's fees. This fee is not prorated if you miss any sessions after you start.
Location + Time:
Sunday mornings TBD/dd>
Coaches:
Our Pumas Coaches will be the skills program coaches. We will have at least one per court but try to have two per court.
USAV Registration:
Participants must be a full registered USAV member to participate. After you register the club director will send you information on the USAV membership available to you.
Program Registration:
You can register for the program anytime on our registration page. Make sure you are signing up for the In-house skills program. There is no tryout fee for this program. Registration deadline will be announced once the session is filled.
Session Date:
  • Session 1: TBD but starting in late Dec or early Jan.
Equipment to Bring:
Shorts, t-shirt, volleyball shoes, knee pads (recommended), sweats (if needed), water.
Program Registration:
Will Open on 11/1. There is no fee to register and no cuts made in the program.
