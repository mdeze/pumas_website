2018 Tryout Schedule

Reminders

  • Plaistow YMCA (formerly RAC) - 175 Plaistow Road, Plaistow NH 03865
  • Please arrive 20-30 minutes early for your tryout.
  • Make sure to bring tryout payment (if not paid online), season deposit, medical form and a copy of your USAV card. You will not be allowed to tryout without all of these.
  • This year both Open and Club will be trying out together with only two tryout dates in total.
  • Results will be posted on the web site for the Open teams and 16-18 club teams by 5 pm on November 29th, 2017. The final team results for 12U-15U Club teams will be posted by 5pm November 30th, 2017. Results will be posted by your tryout number.
