"A common concern every year is the cost of fees. The fees for both the Open and Club divisions are comparable to other clubs. Fees are raised to keep pace with our court costs, tournament fees, uniforms and coach retention. We are a non-profit and only raise fees to the amounts needed to run our programs. One of our hallmarks at Pumas are the five extra practice dates that almost no other club has because they practice on Sunday (They miss practice because of the NERVA tournaments). We hope you will stay with Pumas as we continue to improve our programs and give your daughters an experience they will both enjoy and benefit from."

Stephen Boyle, 2017 Club Director

Season

Open Division

The season fee for the Open Division is $1,840, the balance of $1,740 being payable by midnight December 4th, 2017. This includes:

  • Approximately 35 practice sessions (2 per week depending on tournaments)
  • 5 Single day tournaments (NERVA Regional)
  • 4 Multi-day tournaments (Winterfest, Qualifiers, Boston, Atlanta)
  • Uniforms - 2 competition jerseys, 2 shorts, 2 competition socks, shoes, and warm-ups (top + bottom)
  • End of season picnic in May

Club Division

New Practices Increased

We have increased our practices from 17 to 20.

The season fee for the Club Division is $825, the balance of $725 being payable by midnight December 4th, 2017. This includes:

  • Approximately 20 practice weeks (1 per week unless at a multi-day tournament)
  • 5 Single Day tournaments (NERVA Regional)
  • 1 Multi-day tournament (Boston)
  • Any other tournament that they may attend is voluntary and an additional cost that is split between those going. This includes Winterfest, Qualifiers (for some teams), Philadelphia, Albany, Rhode Island.
  • Uniforms - 1 competition jersey, 1 pair shorts
  • End of season picnic in May

Refund Policy

  • The tryout fee is non-refundable, even if the athlete does not attend any tryout.
  • The $100.00 season deposit is refundable if a girl is cut or drops by notifying the CLUB DIRECTOR by letter, email, or phone on or before November 25th, 2017 (Open or Club). Only refunds for medical necessity with a doctor's note will be allowed after these dates.
  • The season deposit is holding a place for the player. Any player placed on a team but not accepting the position will lose their deposit.
  • If a player incurs a season-ending injury (with a doctor's note) before March 1st, 2018, a partial pro-rated refund will be issued. After March 1st, 2018, no refunds will be given.
  • No refund will be given to a player who pays the Open team fee but whose team plays in the Club division because they are still practicing as an Open team.
  • Refunds are not given for an athletes' inability to attend practices or tournaments because of outside scheduling conflicts or lack of interest.
