"A common concern every year is the cost of fees. The fees for both the Open and Club divisions are comparable to other clubs. Fees are raised to keep pace with our court costs, tournament fees, uniforms and coach retention. We are a non-profit and only raise fees to the amounts needed to run our programs.

One of our hallmarks at Pumas are the six extra practice dates that many other clubs do not have because they hold practice on Sunday (These practices are missed due to Sunday NERVA tournaments and Easter) ) while Pumas practices on Saturday. Starting in the 2018-19 season, we have additional practice time for all players with the addition of a second practice (Tuesday). In addition, multi-day tournaments (Winterfest, Qualifiers, Boston) are also included in the season fee.

We hope you will stay with Pumas as we continue to improve our programs and your child an experience they will both enjoy and benefit from."

Stephen Boyle, Club Director

Pumas 2020-2021 Updated Cost and Payment Info

Costs

The extreme effect that COVID has had upon the availability of coaches, tournaments, and the use of facilities has led Pumas to adjust its fee structure for this season. The updated base cost for this season will be $860 for the season. All other court time will be an additional cost on a per player basis.

Base Cost Covers:

  • Saturday practices for 3 hours (30 min agility session, 2.5-hour team practice) from 12/5 to 5/1/21.This is 66 hours of court time.
  • Tuesday midweek practice from 3/2/21 to 4/27/21. this is 12 hours of court time. Midweek practice will be on Tuesday at Lowell Catholic HS.
  • This is for a total of 78 hours of practice time. In a regular non-covid season there would have been 92 hours of scheduled practice time.

Additional Costs When/If Needed:

  • Single Day Scrimmages - These will be scheduled on a Sunday if at all possible and will not be on the NERVA dates already scheduled. These dates will be scheduled depending on other club and court availability. Estimated cost would be between $30 and $60 per player depending if we have a reciprocal date with the other team (go to their facility), or they are just coming to us and will pay half the cost. As soon as dates are set a commitment will be needed for the number of players attending. Total cost will depend on the total number of athletes attending (the more players the cheaper per person it will be).
  • NERVA Scheduled Dates – These are scheduled for the following Sundays (2/21, 3/14, 3/28, 4/11, 5/2). These will be at a $40 per tournament cost.
  • Convention Center Tournaments - These have been postponed to May + June. Estimates would look to be in the range of $90-$140 per player for Boston (3 days) and Spikefest (2 days with hotel stay). This covers the entry fee, coaches fee and coaches lodging; it does not cover players hotel which is the same during a regular season.
  • Extra Skills Sessions - These may be scheduled for Sundays if courts are available and each session would focus on one particular skill for 2 hours. These would be voluntary. Min of 12 players needed and estimated cost would be dependent upon total number of players attending (between $15 - $25 per player)
  • Additional Court Time - Any other court time we can get whether midweek or Sunday would have an additional cost.
  • Uniforms - There would be a $10 uniform fee for the competition jerseys for the scrimmages and convention center tournaments. If black spandex is needed for the convention center tournaments there would be an additional cost of $25.
  • Extended Practice Season – Commitments will be made by 3/13/21 for any players wishing to play in the Boston (May) or Spikefest tournament (June). This will be for practices between 5/3/21 and 6/11/21.

Refund Policy

  • The tryout fee is non-refundable, even if the athlete does not attend any tryout.
  • The $150.00 season deposit is refundable if a girl is cut or drops by notifying the CLUB DIRECTOR by letter, email, or phone on or before the second tryout (for the age group you are trying out for) (Open or Club). Only refunds for medical necessity with a doctor's note will be allowed after these dates.
  • Both player and parent should know before the second tryout whether they want to be considered for placement on a Pumas team. You will lose your deposit if you drop after teams are announced for any reason other than medical.
  • The season deposit is holding a place for the player. Any player placed on a team but not accepting the position will lose their deposit.
  • If a player incurs a season-ending injury (with a doctor's note) before March 1st, 2021, a pro-rated refund will be issued. After March 1st, 2021, no refunds will be given.
  • No refund will be given to a player who pays the Open team fee but whose team plays in the Club division because they are still practicing as an Open team.
  • Refunds are not given for an athletes' inability to attend practices or tournaments because of outside scheduling conflicts or lack of interest.
  • There are no refunds given for inclement weather or facilities closing because of weather related factors.

COVID Refunds

Any practice or tournaments cancelled by the facility, or the state, because of health and safety factors associated with COVID19 will result in a refund at the end of the season.
